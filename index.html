01 Core Features

What the tool does

Rule-based automation and analytics designed to reduce wasted ad spend and scale what's working.

⚡

Automatic Bid Optimization

Adjusts bids in real-time based on performance signals to maximize ROI across all campaigns.

📊

Keyword Performance Tracking

Monitors keyword-level metrics and surfaces insights to focus spend on high-converting terms.

🛑

Stop-Loss Rules

Automatically pauses or limits spend on non-performing keywords before they drain the budget.

🔍

Search Term Analysis

Analyzes search term reports to identify new opportunities and eliminate irrelevant traffic.

🎯

ACOS-Based Adjustments

Uses target ACOS thresholds to automatically scale bids up or down per campaign goal.

🔄

Rule-Based Automation

Configurable automation rules that run on a schedule, reducing manual campaign management time.
